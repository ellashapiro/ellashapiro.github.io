JG Melon

1291 3rd Ave, New York, NY 10021

A New York Staple

JG Melon is a New York staple. Since being founded on the Upper East Side of Manhattan in 1972 it has become one of the most popular and known restaurants in New York.

My Review (10/10)

The Experience

I love dining at JG Melon! Because it is such a famous establishment you never know who you'll see when you dine there! The restaurant itself is very small so if you go on a Friday/Saturday expect to wait for your table, but it is well worth the wait! Everyone who works there has been there for a long time, so the entire dining experience has an old-timey feel to it. Every decoration in the restaurant is watermelon themed and it is always smokey and smells of their delicious burgers!

JG Melon Menu

JG Melon has a rather small menu, but everything on it is delicious! They like to keep things simple. Burgers and Fries, Sandwiches and Steaks and that's about it!

My Order

My favorite thing to order at JG Melon is a Medium-Done Cheeseburger with Lettuce, Tomatoes and Pickles! If I go with someone we will almost always also share an order of cottage fries, and I exclusively drink Pink Lemonade when I'm there.
